Théâtres Fantômes

Théatres Fantômes is an immersive sound installation of the Théâtre Electronique collective created for the Belgian sound festival City Sonics in Mons. Inspired by haunted places and ghost stories, the installation takes the form of mobiles suspended in the half-light, whose appearance oscillates between an abandoned theater and a shipwreck.

Yann Rocher (design & manufacturing), Antoine Fontaine (manufacturing), Galdric Fleury (manufacturing), Carl Seleborg (voice),Grégoire Carpentier (composition, electronics).
