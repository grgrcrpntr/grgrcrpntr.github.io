Edelweiss

Edelweiss Quartet blends the lyrical strength of the classical romantic repertoire into the rythmical thrust of rock and modern jazz. Both freely revisiting Schubert’s chamber pieces and developing their own compositions, the combo delivers a sensitive, powerful, and very personal music.

Thomas Koenig (sax), Juanjo Burred (piano), Grégoire Carpentier (bass, compositions), Romain Bessin (drums).

Listen: Bandcamp, Deezer, Apple Music, Amazon Music, Spotify.
